    About

    Hi! I made Nhuly TRAN2LATED to have the most accurate translations--as well as to improve on my Vietnamese!



    How To Use:

  • To enter an English word for translation: CTRL+A or CMD+A or File -> Add Word

  • To delete by row: Navigate to the last (third) column and click on the red X of the row you want to delete
  • To clear all: CTRL+K or CMD+K or File -> Clear All


    • Note:

  • Expand screen from top to bottom for the best format
  • Formatting is according to desktops only (not mobile)
  • The add word window will also be in full screen if you have the app open in full screen
  • Over 600 English words have been translated on here
  • Use singular nouns if plural does not work! Since Vietnamese does not have plural words, there is no need for them when translating to Vietnamese
  • Use present tense if past or future tense does not work! There are no past tense words in Vietnamese
